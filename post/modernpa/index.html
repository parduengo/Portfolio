Modern Public Administration

3 minutes
April 7, 2022

Modern Public Administration and the Citizen’s Sovereignty

The challenges

The new challenges of Public Administration are infinite, the constant humanitarian crisis, wars, the globalization, are changing the society.

The questions

What kind of response, how can we help, or which are the needs?

The Answers

The recurrent thoughts are that we need to align and define a new path to fight against monopolies, Governments need to serve equal opportunities and distribute wealth. Different perspectives and experiences about the same problem, it’ll help us to resolve better these challenges. But It’s not a global solution. New proposal to reduce the “time-to-market”, from the needs to the value solutions must come to speed up how it’s resolved quickly. The next point lets us what it must do to achieve the service’s vocation of public administration.

Call-to-action

Technology as a bridge:

The digital or no digital services must be based on technology, but not all services can be digital. It’s needed to define a hybrid service between human and technology. Both taxonomy of services: people-for-people (P4P), and the new administration-for-citizen (A4C). The second one is a superset that contain both things. It can possess a big tool chain to invest less money and develop, it’s the MPV (Minimum Product Viable), this prototyped is like private business based on GAANF, FAANG phenomenon. Pilots, partial improves with new features are some the ingredients to develop and development new platform of services.

Data as “axis mundi”

Data-centric pattern and different kind of architectures must be trust, accessible, flexible, and elastic. The edge between public or private data is a complex balance that evolves all players that manage, get, store, the most important in this new culture surrounding the digital services. Inner this key point, whole public data must be sharing to their citizens because transparency is the cornerstone in this point of view. The next step must be about to develop competitiveness and wealthy.

From the citizens to citizens, public administration and private business must be join forces creating new value services. European Union are working in these ways of developing.

In my opinion: - “This is the way”

This summary is about the survivor’s succeed, it’s like the film and series about Star Wars “Saga”. Don’t put off your helmet and work hard – the next generations and the future are in our hands to modelled it. All innovation and transformation are related with the present and future of digital services and in the next post, I’ll continue writing to develop my ideas with personal interviews to friends and colleagues.

Keep be safe and enjoy!!!
